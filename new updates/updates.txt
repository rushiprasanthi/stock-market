## 3️⃣ “Moderate intraday range” sentence — SHOULD WE KEEP IT?

### Original sentence:

> “The stock traded within a moderate intraday range and closed below its opening price…”

### Reality:

❌ “moderate” = interpretation
❌ “closed below” = okay, but combined with wording becomes narrative

### ✅ Better TRUST-SAFE replacement:

```text
The stock traded between its recorded intraday high and low values and closed below its opening price.
```

OR even cleaner:

```text
Intraday price range and open–close values are shown below.
```

👉 **No adjectives = no bias**

## 5️⃣ 🔥 MOST IMPORTANT IDEA (Your point #2) — THIS IS GOLD

> “If today’s price matches any past day price
> and today it falls
> show that past day’s major news”

This is **excellent**, and still **judgement-free**.

### Why this is VALID

* You are **not saying cause**
* You are saying **historical coincidence**
* User makes judgement

### TRUST-SAFE wording:

```text
Today's closing price matches a previously observed closing price.

For reference, major news reported on that previous date is listed below.
No causal relationship is implied.
```

### Example:

```text
Matching historical price:
• 256.44 on 2026-01-29

Major news reported on that date:
• Apple faces supply-chain delays in China – Reuters
• Global markets fall on rate-hike concerns – Reuters
```

👉 This is **data context**, NOT analysis.

📘 PROJECT DOCUMENTATION
Daily Stock Summary Report

(Trust-First | Reference-Only | Investor-Grade)

1. Project Overview
Project Title

Daily Stock Summary Report Generator

Domain

Financial Data Reporting / Investor Support Tool

Objective

To generate a daily, trust-safe stock summary report that presents observed price data and relevant news in a clean, structured PDF format — without analysis, prediction, or recommendations.

2. Problem Statement

Small and independent investors often rely on multiple sources such as trading apps, charts, and news websites to understand daily stock movements. This process is:

Time-consuming

Fragmented

Mentally exhausting

Prone to missing critical context

While large financial platforms provide comprehensive insights, they are expensive, complex, and often over-analytical for daily monitoring needs.

3. Proposed Solution

This project provides an automated daily stock report that consolidates:

Observed daily price data

Short-term historical price references

Stock-specific news

Market and geopolitical context

All information is presented in a trust-safe, non-interpretive manner.

4. Design Philosophy (Core Principle)
Golden Rule

Expose the data, hide the judgement

The report:

Shows what happened

Never explains why it happened

Never suggests what to do next

This ensures:

Investor neutrality

Legal safety

High trust

5. Intended Users
Primary Users

Retail investors

Long-term / positional investors

Busy professionals monitoring markets daily

Not Intended For

Intraday traders

Signal-based trading

Buy / sell decision making

6. Report Structure (Final Version)
1️⃣ Price Snapshot (Observed Data)

Open price

Close price

Day high (with time)

Day low (with time)

Intraday range

All values are reported exactly as observed.

2️⃣ Today vs Last 5 Trading Days (Reference Table)

Purpose:

Shows where today’s price sits relative to recent days.

Characteristics:

Absolute numerical differences only

No labels such as “higher”, “lower”, “better”, or “worse”

3️⃣ Sequential Daily Price Change (Math-Only)

Purpose:

Shows day-to-day price movement continuity.

Characteristics:

Close price vs previous trading day

Mathematical difference only

No interpretation or conclusion

4️⃣ 5-Day Price Extremes (Observed)

Purpose:

Displays recent price boundaries.

Contents:

Highest observed closing price (5 days)

Lowest observed closing price (5 days)

Exact dates included

5️⃣ Historical Price Match + News Reference (Conditional)

Purpose:

If today’s closing price matches a previous closing price,
relevant news from that historical date is shown.

Important Note:

No causal relationship is implied.

News is shown for reference only.

6️⃣ Company-Specific News

News published within the selected time window

Trusted sources only

If no material news exists, this is explicitly stated

7️⃣ Market & Geopolitical Context

Selected macro-level and geopolitical news

Included for environmental awareness

Explicitly marked as non-stock-specific

8️⃣ Methodology & Trust Boundary

The report clearly states:

Data sources used

Time cut-off applied

No analysis, prediction, or advice is provided

Comparisons are for reference only

9️⃣ Intended Use Statement

The report is designed for:

Daily monitoring

Market awareness

Reference documentation

Not intended for:

Trading decisions

Forecasting

Sentiment guidance

7. Data Sources

Price data: Public market data providers

News data: Trusted RSS sources (e.g., Reuters, Yahoo Finance)

Charts: Generated from observed price data

All data is:

Publicly available

Time-filtered

Presented without modification

8. Trust & Compliance Considerations

No subjective language

No sentiment labels

No forward-looking statements

Clear disclaimers included

This makes the report:

Investor-neutral

Legally safer

Ethically sound

Why It Works

10. Key Strengths

11. Limitations (By Design)
