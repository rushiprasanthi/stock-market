Problem Statement (Professional Rewrite)

Small and independent market participants often rely on multiple sources—trading apps, news websites, and charts—to understand daily stock movements. This process is time-consuming, fragmented, and repetitive. While large financial platforms provide comprehensive reports, they are expensive, complex, and not tailored for individual needs.

Existing solutions offer pieces of the required information (prices, charts, or news), but there is a lack of a simple, customizable, and automated daily reporting tool designed specifically for small users who want a clear overview without analysis or predictions.


---

Proposed Solution

The solution is a lightweight automation tool that generates a daily stock summary report in PDF or DOC format, containing:

Daily price information (open, high, low, close)

Simple, readable charts

Related news headlines published on the same day

A clean, consistent report structure delivered every day


This tool focuses on data organization and clarity, not predictions or investment advice. It is intended for a small group of users who value regular, structured information and want to reduce manual effort.


---

Scope & Reality Check

This solution:

Is designed for small users, not mass markets

Prioritizes trust, consistency, and simplicity

Can be built, deployed, and maintained realistically

Can generate steady, modest income

Is not intended to replace large financial platforms or provide trading signals
